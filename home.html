WELCOME

SECRETS OF SUCCESS
OF OUR PROJECTS

S. K. Samanta & Co. (P) Ltd., better known as SKSL, is an ISO 9001-2008 engineering company that has been executing EPC contracts in energy and infrastructure sectors since its incorporation in 1982. We have a long history of executing of infrastructural projects like bulk material handling plants, heavy industrial workshop complexes, cross country pipelines, roads, educational complexes, buildings, etc. With a passion for growth, SKSL has recently diversified into the steel sector by setting up a fully automated TMT Bar Rolling Mill with an impressive capacity of 500 MT/day. We firmly believe in our commitment to provide sustainable solutions will have a lasting impact on communities across India.
