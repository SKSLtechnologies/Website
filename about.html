ABOUT US

Home About Us

S. K. Samanta & Co. (P) Ltd., better known as SKSL, is an ISO 9001-2008 engineering company that has been executing EPC contracts in energy and infrastructure sectors since its incorporation in 1982. During the last four decades, SKSL is engaged in the execution of turnkey projects right from the underlying stages of planning, design, procurement, engineering, supply, construction to commissioning and handing over to clients after conducting performance guarantee tests. We are a people-driven enterprise with a team of incredibly competent and experienced engineers from various industries who strive for the common goal of excellence. With a passion for growth, SKSL has recently diversified into the steel sector by setting up a fully automated TMT Bar Rolling Mill with an impressive capacity of 500 MT/day. We firmly believe in our commitment to provide sustainable and ingenious solutions that have a lasting impact on communities across India by leveraging our extensive engineering expertise, our ethos of innovation, and our cutting edge technology.

ABOUT THE COMPANY

Mission

With a focus on time and quality, SKSL continually strives to deliver excellence by adopting state-of-the-art technologies, methodologies and materials in the field of engineering, designing, construction, operation and maintenance of EPC projects.

For immaculate and on-time execution of projects, we leverage strategic collaborations with diverse professionals to respond to our clients' needs in an innovative, economical, efficient,and dynamic manner.

SKSL is committed to continuing its rich legacy of seamless partnerships with its prolific clients and customer base by conducting our business with integrity, hard work and transparent practices.

We encourage a safe working environment in a way that fosters learning, development and appreciation for high-calibre and passionate individuals.

ABOUT THE COMPANY

Vision

To emerge as a distinguished leader in all avenues of business that we venture into with our people, with our customers being an integral part of the process.

To forge trusted, long-lasting relationships and adhere to the mantra of execution excellence driven by an entrepreneurial spirit and a sense of accountability to serve our clients.

To expand our core competencies to guarantee that our projects are mutually beneficial for all without compromising on our responsibility as engineers.

To endeavour to meet the needs of today without jeopardising the ability of future generations to by integrating feasible, sustainable development practices and generally striving towards creating a better nation.
